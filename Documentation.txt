2. PACKAGE CONTENTS
Inside the ZIP file, you will find:
- index.html: The main structure of the website.
- style.css: Custom styles and design configurations.
- script.js: Interactivity and typing animations.
- img/: Folder for your profile picture and project screenshots.
- README.txt: This documentation file.

---

3. CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

A. Change Name and Bio:
Open index.html and search for "Ariq Azmain". Replace it with your own name.
Example: <h1>Hi, I am [Your Name]</h1>

B. Update Profile Picture:
Place your photo inside the "img" folder. In index.html, update the image source:
Example: <img src="img/your-photo.jpg" alt="Profile Picture">

C. Adding New Projects:
To add a new project, copy the following block inside the projects section of index.html:

<div class="col-md-4">
    <div class="card">
        <img src="img/project-thumbnail.jpg" class="card-img-top" alt="Project Name">
        <div class="card-body">
            <h5 class="card-title">Project Title</h5>
            <p class="card-text">Short description of your work.</p>
            <a href="#" class="btn btn-primary">View Project</a>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
