Element Details

H

Hydrogen

Atomic Number: 1

Atomic Mass: 1.008

Category: Nonmetal

Properties

Period: 1
Group: 1
Block: s
State: Gas
Density: 0.0899 g/L
Melting Point: -259.16°C
Boiling Point: -252.87°C
Electronegativity: 2.20

Description

Hydrogen is the lightest and most abundant chemical element in the universe. It is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, non-toxic, and highly combustible diatomic gas.

Common Uses

Used in petroleum refining, ammonia production, metal refining, and as a rocket fuel. Also being explored as a clean energy source in fuel cells.
